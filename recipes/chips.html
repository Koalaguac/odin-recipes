Air Fryer Sweet Potato Chips Recipe

Homepage

Description

An Air Fryer has so many cool uses, one of them are Potato Chips.

Everyone loooves Chips, right? Well here is a healthy alternative which still tastes great!

And the good part is, you'll only need 30 min to make them!

Ingredients

  • 1 large Sweet potato
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper.
  • cooking spray

Step-By-Step guide

  1. Slice Potato into thin slices
  2. Put slices into large bowl of cold water; soak for 20 minutes
  3. Drain and pat dry with paper towels
  4. Put slices into bowl. Add oil, salt, and pepper
  5. Preheat Air Fryer to 175Â°C
  6. Cook until crispy and golden, 12 to 16 minutes
  7. Let them dry. Done!
