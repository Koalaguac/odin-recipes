Air Fryer Chicken Tenders Recipe

Homepage

Description

An Air Fryer has so many cool uses, one of them is Chicken Tender.

Everyone loooves Chicken, right? Well here is a great way to cook it without a pan!

And the good part is, you'll only need 30 min to make them!

Ingredients

  • 1 large Egg
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 8 chicken tenders
  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs.
  • cooking spray
  • chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs.

Step-By-Step guide

  1. Preheat Air Fryer to 175°C. Coat basket with cooking spray
  2. Put slices into large bowl of cold water; soak for 20 minutes
  3. Drain and pat dry with paper towels
  4. Put slices into bowl. Add oil, salt, and pepper
  5. Preheat Air Fryer to 175°C
  6. Cook until crispy and golden, 12 to 16 minutes
  7. Let them dry. Done!
